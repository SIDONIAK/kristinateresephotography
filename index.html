About Me

Hi, I’m Kristina, the face behind Kristina Terese Photography. Two of my greatest passions are traveling and photographing. I've been doing both since a young age and my love for them both only keeps growing. I started out my photography career thinking I wanted to do travel photography, but since dabbling in all sorts of other photography, I've realized my favorite subject is people - whether it’s a family session, a portrait, or even a sporting competition, I love capturing the little moments that people will cherish forever.
